Game Rules

Objective

Move around the canteen, pick up items lying on the floor, and throw them into appropriate bins. The more you sort correctly, the cleaner and greener the canteen gets!

Gameplay

  • Sorting Items: Identify garbage items like food wrappers, plastic bottles, cans, and food leftovers, then sort them into the correct bins.
  • Challenge: The more items you sort correctly, the quicker the canteen becomes eco-friendly. Be cautious of mix-up items that require extra care!

Game Over

Once everything is sorted and accounted for, the canteen is cleaned up, and you’re an Eco Champion – well done on keeping your college green!
